Welcome to Dota

Goal of Dota

In each game, you control a hero. Your goal is to make your hero stronger by gaining levels and items, then taking your team's 5 power-up heroes and destroying the enemy ancient.


Heroes are split up by attributes. There are Strength, Agility, and Intelligence. For heroes that don't belong in any attribute, they are moved to Universal.
