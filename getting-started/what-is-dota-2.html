What is Dota 2?

Goal of the Dota

Your goal as a team is to destroy the enemy's ancient first. Stopping you is the enemy team, who are playing for the same goal. Playing Dota 2 consists of making plays that bring you closer to destroying the enemy's base while making sure your base stays safe.

Each team is filled with 5 roles.
