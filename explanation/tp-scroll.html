TP Scroll

Town Portal Scrolls, also called TP Scrolls, allow units to teleport to friendly buildings after a channeling time.

Heroes receive a free TP Scroll when they die.

After the first TP, every consecutive TP will have extra channeling time.

Every hero gets a TP slot in their inventory. TP scrolls require to be purchased to be used.

TP costs 75 mana to use.

Buying Boots of Travel allows you to teleport onto creeps. BoTs also lower the cooldown to 40s.
