Water Rune

Link

Water runes spawn at both rune spots in the river at 2:00 and 4:00. They do not spawn again for the rest of the game.

Bounty Rune

Link

Bounty runes spawn at all rune spots at the start of the game (0:00), and then every 3 minutesin the designated bounty rune spots in both jungles.

Wisdom Rune

Link

Wisdom runes spawn at the designated wisdom rune spots every 7 minutes from the start of the game.

Power Runes

Power runes spawn in the designated river rune spots every 2 minutes starting at 6:00 (8:00, 10:00, 12:00, and so on).
