Map

A team is filled with 5 roles, each with a second name:

  • Carry/Position 1
  • Mid/Position 2
  • Offlane/Position 3
  • Support/Position 4
  • Hard Support/Position 5

Hero Controls

Dealing the killing blow will give you gold. Each hero has their unique attack animation and deal different damage. Players will have to get use to attacking with their hero to time the attack to be the final hit.
