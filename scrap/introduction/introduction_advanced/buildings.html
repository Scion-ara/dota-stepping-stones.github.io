What sites to use Basics and Fundamentals Advanced

Buildings

Basics

Towers

  • Each lane has 3 tiers of towers
  • Towers can be referenced by shorthand
  • Outer towers need to be destroyed before towers in the same lane can be damaged
  • There are two tier 4 towers, both of which need to be destroyed before the ancient can be damaged

Barracks

  • Each lane have 2 barracks - melee and range
  • Destroying one barracks will buff your creeps in that lane, turning them into mega creeps
  • Destroying all barracks will upgrade your creeps into super mega creeps
  • Barracks can only be damaged after the nearby tier 3 tower has been destroyed

Ancient

  • The most important building
  • Game only ends once one of the ancients gets destroyed
  • The ancient can only be damaged after tier 4 towers have fallen
